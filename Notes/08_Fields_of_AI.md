##Fields of Artificial Intelligence

1. Machine Learning(ML)
-> It is a field of AI that enables computer to to learn from data and make prediction without being explicitly programmed.

2. Deep Learning(DL)
-> It is a subset of Machine Learning that uses neural networks to solve complex problems such as image and speech recognition.

3. Computer Vision
-> It allows computers to understand and analyze images and videos.

4. Natural language Processing(NLP)
-> It enables machines to understand, interpret and generate human language.

5. Robotics
-> It combines AI with machines to perform tasks in the physical world.

6. Expert Systems
-> It is an AI program that simulate the decision making ability of human expert.


## CONCLUSION

These fields work together to create intelligent systems capable of learning, reasoning, understanding, and interacting with humans.
