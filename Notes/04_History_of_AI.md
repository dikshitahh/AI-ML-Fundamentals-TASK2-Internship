##History of Artificial Intelligence

-> In the Year 1950, Alan Turing proposed a test named The Turing Test.

-> In the Year 1956, the term Artificial Intelligence was introduced by John McCarthy at the Dartmouth Conference.

-> In the Year 1997, IBM Deep Blue defeated Chess Champion Garry Kasparov.

-> In the Year 2006, Geoffrey Hinton and his team coined the term 'Deep Learning'.

-> (2020-present) comes the Generative AI era in which the modern AI models can generate text, images, audio and code.
